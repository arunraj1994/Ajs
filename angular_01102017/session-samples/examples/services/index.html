Differences between Services, Factory and Providers

  • Services and Factory are almost the same. Both can be injected inside the controller/services to share data
  • While injecting service, we are creating a new instance of the service
  • While injecting factory, we are returning the object already created
  • There is no stuff that service can do but factory can't and also factory can do but service can't
  • Providers can be injected in controllers, services and config whereas services and factory can't be injected in config
